Our Outreach

Bringing Safe Water to Communities

At Maji Kwa Watoto Foundation, we provide clean drinking water to schools, orphanages, and centers across Kenya’s coastal region. Through partnerships with Dutch Water Ltd and local agencies, we promote hygiene and children’s rights, building healthier communities with support from Stichting Waterpas Foundation.

Our Values

These values guide our work and reflect our commitment to children, schools, and communities.

Why These Values Matter

In coastal Kenya, many children face salty or unsafe drinking water. That is why Inclusion means no child is left thirsty in school. Accountability means staying transparent with our partners, like Stichting Waterpas in the Netherlands. Clean Water reflects our mission with New Dutch Water, whose promise is “Healthy drinking water for all people.” And Growth reminds us to keep expanding so more communities can benefit tomorrow.
